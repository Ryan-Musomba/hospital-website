Medical Services

General Consultation

Our general consultation services provide expert medical advice and diagnosis for common health concerns. Whether it's a routine check-up or a new health issue, our doctors are here to help.

Pediatric Care

We offer specialized healthcare for children, including routine check-ups, vaccinations, and treatment for common childhood illnesses.

Emergency Services

Our emergency department is available 24/7 to provide immediate care for critical health conditions and accidents.

Laboratory Testing

We offer a variety of diagnostic tests, including blood tests, urine analysis, and imaging, to ensure accurate diagnosis and treatment.

Physiotherapy

Our physiotherapy services help patients recover from injuries, surgeries, and chronic conditions through specialized exercises and therapies.

Mental Health Support

We provide counseling and psychiatric services for individuals dealing with stress, anxiety, depression, and other mental health concerns.
